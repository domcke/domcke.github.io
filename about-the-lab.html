Our lab

Our lab is part of the Department of Molecular Life Sciences at the University of Zurich. Combining the natural beauty of lakes and mountains with academic excellence and a high quality of life, Zurich attracts global talent for both work and living.

Founded in the fall of 2024, we are an interdisciplinary team of wet lab, dry lab and 'hybrid' scientists. We value a collegial and collaborative lab atmosphere, where everyone has the resources and support needed to reach their full potential.

Join Us

We welcome aspiring and established scientists of all ages, seniorities and career paths. If you are interested in joining our lab, please contact Silvia with a statement of motivation and your CV.

PhD students in our lab are part of a joint PhD program with the ETH Zurich. You can either apply through the Systems Biology or Molecular Life Sciences program, or contact Silvia directly.
